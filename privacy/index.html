Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Free Web Tools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our tools.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal data. All tools run entirely in your browser (client-side). No text, images, files, or data you enter is sent to our servers or stored by us.

However, third-party services we use may collect certain information:

  • Ad Networks (Adsterra): Our advertising partner may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads. They may collect non-personal information such as browser type, device type, IP address, and browsing patterns.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data such as pages visited, time spent, and browser information. Google's Privacy Policy

How We Use Information

Information collected by third parties is used for:

  • Serving relevant advertisements
  • Analyzing site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improving our tools and user experience

Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Ad Personalization: Adsterra uses cookies to deliver relevant ads based on your interests.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to track site usage.
  • Consent Storage: We store your GDPR cookie consent preference in your browser's localStorage. This is not a cookie but a local storage item that never leaves your browser.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. You may also decline cookies via our GDPR consent banner on your first visit.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Adsterra — Ad delivery and monetization
  • Google Analytics — Usage analytics
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting
  • jsDelivr CDN — For loading jsQR library on the QR Code Reader page

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

Data Security

Since all tool processing happens client-side in your browser, there is no data transmission, storage, or processing on our servers. This minimizes data security risks significantly.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — Request information about data processed about you
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data (note: we store none)
  • Right to restrict processing — Restrict how your data is used
  • Right to object — Object to data processing for advertising
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our GitHub profile.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via GitHub.
